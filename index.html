Design guidelines

A set of resources and guidance to ensure that the Prosper brand is consistent across mutliple platforms and mediums. Please use the #branding channel on Slack if you have any comments or questions.

Prosper Logo

There are several versions of the Prosper logo. The key components being the logo mark, the logotype and a combination of the two.

The logo sits best on dark teal and warm grey.

Typography

The main font is Museo Sans Rounded. It's flexible, soft and can evoke SUBTLETY and STRENGTH through its six weights.

Long-form content uses Rooney Web, this compliments Museo Rounded and retains similar characteristics.
